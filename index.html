Who Can Apply?

Requirements:

  • 18 -24 Years old
  • GED/High School Diploma
  • Of low or moderated income
  • U.S. Citizen, permanent resident, or have an employment authorization card
  • Available Monday through Friday for a full year program
  • Motivated to learn both technical and professional skills

What Year Up Offers?

  • Development of Skills
  • Earn Credits
  • Support/Guidance
  • Meeting Mentors/Coaches

What is Year Up?

Year Up is an intensive one-year Informational Technology Program and during the first five months of learning and development phase. Trainees will go through trainings such as web development, professional skills, business communications, technical skills both hardware and software.

What happens after?

After five months which consist of three different modules. The trainee will go into internship where they would put the skills that they've learned to work and to make their 'professional brand' known in other words to have great reputation to those who doesn't know the individual. They can get a glimpse of who they are through the brand they've held through the L&D phase.

What happens after internship?

After internship there are three ways of following up of what happens afterwards.

  • 1st There's a chance of being hired with the company you are interning for
  • 2nd - Applying for other jobs with other tech companies
  • 3rd - Would be entering a career outside of tech
